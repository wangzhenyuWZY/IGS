Frequently asked questions

  • What is GoldenEggs.finance?
    Golden Eggs is a DeFi ecosystem composed of two Defi Tokens and many Farming contracts, hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource.
  • What does Chickens do?
    Golden Chickens produces Golden Eggs (GLDE) continuously, and distributes them among all Chickens holders.
    By holding Chickens, you will get Golden Eggs (GLDE) everyday, forever. 
    When a player get Chickens, half his TRX are used to buy GLDE from Justswap, the other half are used to add liquidity (among with the just bought GLDE) to the GLDE/TRX exchange liquidity pool.
    This gives great support and value to Golden Eggs (GLDE) token.

    This gives great support and value to Golden Diamonds (GLDI) token.

  • What about Gnomes?
    Golden Gnomes produces Golden Diamonds (GLDI) continuously, and distributes them among all Gnomes holders.
    By holding Gnomes, you will get Golden Diamonds (GLDI) everyday, forever.
    Unlike with Chickens Bunnies and Gooses, Gnomes can be only acquired by hatching (consuming) Golden Eggs.
    You can produce GLDE with farming or you can buy them from the Golden DEX or Justswap at any time.
    When you hatch your GLDE to get Gnomes, such GLDE are locked forever (burned), this support the value of GLDE tokens in a passive way (since burned GLDE cannot be sold to exchange anymore).

  • Can i sell Chickens, Gooses, Bunnies Gnomes or Fairies?

  • How is the value of Chickens, Gooses, Bunnies, Gnomes, Dragons and Fairies calculated?
    Chickens value is same as GLDE/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDE pool in justswap).
    Gooses value is same as GLDI/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDI pool in justswap).
    Bunnies value is same as GLDE/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDE pool in justswap).
    Flamingos value is same as GLDI/TRX LP value (liquidity tokens of GLDI pool in justswap).
    Gnomes value is same as GLDE value (1 Gnome = 1 GLDE).
    Dragons value is same as GLDE + GLDI value (1 Dragon = 1 GLDE + 1 GLDI).
    Fairies value is same as GLDI value (1 Fairy = 1 GLDI).
  • What is the difference between Animals Farms and Magical Farms?
    Animals Farms (Chickens, Gooses, Bunnies, Flamingos, Penguins) are acquired by spending TRX, those TRX are injected into the GLDE and GLDI liquidity pools. These liquidity pool  release a special kind of token (Liquidity Tokens), that are the Animals. Thus, Animals value are tied to liquidity. Since liquidity in Goldeneggs Dapp is locked by default, Animals value tend to remain quite stable.
    Magical Farms (Gnomes, Fairies, Dragons) are acquired by spending Tokens (GLDE or GLDI), those Tokens are locked forever in the farm contract (burned). Thus, Magical Entities (Gnomes, Fairies, Dragons) value is tied to the Tokens value. Since GLDE and GLDI price depend exclusively from supply and demand (how the community interact with the tokens), their value tend to be more volatile and much less stable compared to Animals (LP tokens) value.
    Animals are much safer and tend to offer a lower APY.
    Magicals are more riskious but offer higher APY. 
  • What do Golden Peacock and Golden Toucan do?
    Golden Peacock and Golden Toucan are the NFT (non functional token) of GoldenEggs ecosystem!
    There is only ONE Peacock and ONE Toucan, and they can only have one owner per time.
    Anytime the Peacock or Toucan is bought, 90% of trx spent by the new owner goes to refund the previous owner! The rest of trx are used for provide liquidity to GLDI or GLDE.
    Anytime a new owner buy the Peacock or Toucan, its price increase of +15%!
    Everyday the price decrease of -1.5%.
    Peacock and Toucan produces GLDI and GLDE every day for their owners.

About Golden Eggs

Golden Eggs is a DeFi ecosystem composed of two Defi Tokens and three Farming contracts, hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource. It is the first Defi Dapp that is easy to use, thanks to the "One Click Staking" function, and safe thanks to it's embedded secured-liquidity mechanism. All liquidity is always locked forever, by default. This offers a great protection against impermanent losses also known as "rug pull", since nobody can take advantage of the system. Golden Eggs and Golden Diamonds price is totally dictated by supply and demand dynamics, the whole dapp is fully decentralized all the time. Golden Eggs can be bought and sold at any time, and they can be also used to farm Golden Diamonds! Golden Diamonds can be bought and sold at any time!
